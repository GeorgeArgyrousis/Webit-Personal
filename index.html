People seem to like programming because it allows them to instruct a machine. I like programming because it allows me to create. Webit is the vision of my life’s work being uploaded to the web, made accessible to millions of people, and providing them with value.

Every project that I involve myself with, I envision it as something that I would use on an everyday basis. It takes decisive action and enormous devotion to break through any unforeseen barriers that may come along. But this is what it takes to see a project successfully realised.
